Why Best of Tamil Tweeps?

A few years back, I was joined twitter.com and found it the best place to be than any other social media sites. here what makes unique is the quality of the people I follow. I'm here for over the years, but to my surprise still there are lots of the high-quality twitter handles who used to post interestingly in Tamil. So we want to make it the easiest process to follow the high-quality Tamil handles with this one-stop curated platform.

About Best of Tamil Tweeps

Dear Tweeps.,

Best of Tamil Tweeps is a gender-neutral living directory of contentful Tamil people. Our mission is to provide a space to find outstanding people to follow, look for a mentor, make new friends, or discover talented, fun, interesting, experienced, knowledgeable Tamil people to learn from them.

Request an Edit or Opt Out

If you’ve been added to the directory and would like to opt out or make an edit to your profile, please send us a message on Twitter to Best of Tamil Tweeps.
